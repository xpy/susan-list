Pricelist

The prices below are indicative and they may differ depending on the complexity of the background - usually for full body pictures.

The paper I use is Bristol Smooth and I always leave a margin around the drawing.

A frame is not included so you can frame it yourself and use a frame of your choice.

Any decent quality photo is fine, but the size and quality of the portrait is really dependant on the size and quality of the photo. Ideally it should be a close up portrait photo, picking a face from a distant photo can also work but usually the result cannot be bigger than A4.

The best result is of course if you take your time and take a photo especially for the portrait.

The sizes indicated bellow are the sizes of the paper and not the sizes of the portrait, the drawing itself is usually around twenty percent smaller than the paper size, and, needless to say, if your picture is square, the result will also be square.
